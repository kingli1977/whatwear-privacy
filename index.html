Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

We do not collect any personal data. All your wardrobe data stays on your device.

What We Collect

  • Clothing photos — Photos you take or select are stored locally on your device only. We never upload, transmit, or access your photos.
  • Clothing metadata — Category, color, season, and style tags you assign are stored locally.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identity information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash logs
  • Device identifiers

Photo Processing

When you add a clothing item, the app uses the remove.bg API to remove the background:

  • Your photo is sent securely via HTTPS
  • remove.bg processes the image and returns the result
  • We do not store your photos on any server
  • See remove.bg Privacy Policy

Camera & Photo Library

  • Camera — Used to take photos of your clothing items
  • Photo Library — Used to select existing photos and full-body photos for AR preview

Both permissions are optional — you can use the app without granting them.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. No cloud sync. Deleting the app removes all data permanently.

Third-Party Services

  • remove.bg — Background removal API (photo sent for processing, not stored)
  • Apple ARKit — On-device AR clothing preview (iOS only, no data leaves device)

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
